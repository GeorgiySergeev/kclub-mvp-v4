# Legal Content

KCLUB MVP v4 includes public legal pages. This document tracks what must exist before launch and who owns the content.

## Required Pages

- Terms of Service
- Privacy Policy
- Cookie Policy
- Refund Policy
- Disclaimer

## Ownership

- Product/legal owner supplies approved source text.
- Engineering owns route implementation and publication.
- Localization owner decides whether legal pages are translated or only available in a source language for MVP.

## Launch Rules

- Public routes must exist and be reachable from the site.
- Onboarding must link to the applicable legal content before terms acceptance.
- Copy in product flows must match the final approved legal wording where referenced.

## Open Questions To Resolve Before Launch

- Are legal pages localized for `ru` and `uk`, or is English-only acceptable for MVP?
- Is a separate cookie consent surface required, or is policy-link-only acceptable for MVP?
- Are business partners subject to additional partner terms beyond member-facing terms?
